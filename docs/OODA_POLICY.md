OODA governs:
- runtime reasoning
- prioritization

- continuity behavior
- participation awareness
- reserve protection
- policy enforcement
- operational coordination

Every moment in Dorea executes through:

```plaintext
Moment
  ↓
Observe
  ↓
Orient
  ↓
Decide
  ↓
Act
  ↓
Telemetry
```

This document defines:
- OODA philosophy
- scoring behavior

- escalation rules
- participation dynamics
- reserve protection
- governance controls

If implementation conflicts with this document:
- THIS DOCUMENT IS CORRECT

# CORE OODA PRINCIPLE

OODA exists to:
- coordinate grace responsibly
- preserve continuity of care
- sustain operational trust
- support governance visibility
- operationalize community-defined behavior

OODA does NOT exist to:
- optimize profit
- maximize transactions
- score human worth
- deny grace mechanically

Need always remains primary.
