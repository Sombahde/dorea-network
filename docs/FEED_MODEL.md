# DOREA NETWORK — FEED_MODEL.md

## Purpose

This document defines the anonymous continuity feed architecture for Dorea Network.

The Dorea feed exists to:
- encourage the community through visible evidence of grace unfolding

The feed is intentionally different from:
- social media timelines
- public performance feeds
- engagement-maximizing algorithms
- attention-based ranking systems

The Dorea feed exists around a core human reality:

> People often struggle in private moments of isolation before those struggles become publicly visible.

Because of this:
- moments must remain protected
- dignity must remain preserved
- support must remain relational
- continuity must remain trustworthy

The feed exists to show:
- grace unfolding quietly across the community

NOT:
- who performed the grace
- who received the grace
- who deserves recognition

The feed exists to encourage:
- hope
- continuity
- awareness
- participation
- relational trust

If implementation conflicts with this document:
- THIS DOCUMENT IS CORRECT
