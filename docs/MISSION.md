# DOREA NETWORK — MISSION.md

## Mission

Dorea Network exists to ensure that grace is always available through trusted communities connected by prayer, encouragement, support, and acts of mercy.

The network enables communities to coordinate care through invitation, participation, relational trust, and governed runtime orchestration rather than institutional systems, financial optimization, or transactional behavior.

Dorea exists to help communities respond to moments of need with dignity, wisdom, compassion, continuity of care, and always-available grace.

# CORE STATEMENT

Grace is always available.

# CONTRACT-DRIVEN GRACE

Dorea operates through community-defined behavioral contracts.

Contracts define:
- how the community responds
- what guidance should be offered
- who should be notified
- when escalation occurs
- what continuity of care should exist
- how delegated participation authority is governed

Members participate through simple grace contracts.

These contracts define:
- participation preferences
- availability
- escalation permissions
- continuity behaviors
- delegated response authority

Members may refine their contracts at any time.

The purpose of contracts is not enforcement.

The purpose of contracts is to preserve continuity of care through governed delegated participation.

When members are unavailable, governed continuity behaviors may allow Dorea to:
- acknowledge requests
- offer encouragement
- preserve continuity of care
- notify additional participants
- maintain relational presence

No request should be left unanswered.

No member should be left alone.

New capabilities are not primarily created through software development.

New capabilities emerge through:
- community-defined contracts
- governance refinement
- observed moments of care
- evolving participation

The community defines the behavior.

The runtime executes the behavior.
